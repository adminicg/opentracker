minor issues:
- when changing transparency to 0 (no transparency) it can happen for Cubes, Spheres, 
  Cones and Cylinders, that the transparency keeps sticking at 0 and does not change anymore.

  can be changed again.
  In order to change the transparency in case of the plane (!) you have to update/drag it
  right in the beginning. Changing the transparency in this case will result in a loss of
  all intersection curves (no intersections will be shown anymore if transparency is <> 0)
